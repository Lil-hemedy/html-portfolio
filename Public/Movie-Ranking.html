My top 5 movies of all time

The Black List

A wanted fugitive mysteriously surrenders himself to the FBI and offers to help them capture deadly criminals. His sole condition is that he will only with the profiler, Elizabeth Keen .

The 100

A nuclear conflict has decimated civilisation. A century later, a spaceship accommodating humanity's lone survivors dispatch 100 juvinile delinquents back to the earth to determine its habitability .

Game of thrones

Nine noble families wage war agaist each other in order to gain control over the mythical land of westeros. Meanwhile a force is rising after milleniums and threatens the existence of living men .

The walking dead

In the wake of a zombie apocalypse, various survivors struggle to stay alive. As they search for safety and evade the undead, they are forced to grapple with rival groups and difficult choices .

The last kingdom

During their invasion of England, the Danes capture Uhtred, a young successor of Saxon earldom, and raise him as their own. Years later, Uhtred's loyalties are put to the test by the Danes .
